WHAT IS SMI PLAY?

SMI Play (powered by Enjin) is an online platform that allows users to earn cryptocurrency by playing games via play-to-earn. In-game token items are stored as Fungible or Non-Fungible Tokens on the JumpNet blockchain, you can either use them in games, sell on a marketplace or melt them to destroy them for cryptocurrency.

SMI Play will launch with one game available - Moonshot Voyage, with more games to join it shortly after.

ROLE OF SMI

Users can use their Ethereum address as an account to access any game on the platform, the account is shared between games.

SMI had its ownership renounced, which makes it virtually impossible to change contract code. Original SMI is still used on the platform - games can read the SMI amount on players' accounts. SMI holders get in-game benefits from holding the token, accordingly to the amount held. The more SMI you hold, the better chance of obtaining a rare Token item you have. If you hold above a certain amount of SMI, you can also get a special Fish/Dolphin/Whale status for additional benefits, free item rewards etc.

TOKEN ITEMS

  • Token Items can be obtained in a variety of ways:
    ‚Ä¢ Buying - you can buy an item directly from the marketplace
    ‚Ä¢ Dropping - you can get an item via gameplay, as a random drop
    ‚Ä¢ Reward - you can get an item as a free reward for holding SMI, via the Fish/Dolphin/Whale status

  • Every Token Item you have can be used in game, sold on the marketplace or melted to gain a set amount of ENJ cryptocurrency. Every transaction using SMI Play games Token Items includes a 4% fee that goes straight into the community dev wallet.

MELTING

Melting a Token Item is a process of taking it out of the blockchain in exchange for a set amount of ENJ cryptocurrency - essentially destroying the item for ENJ. Melting includes a 40% fee - player receives 60% of the Token Item's melt value, 40% goes to the community dev wallet.

ITEM RARITY

There are 6 tiers of item rarity in SMI Play games:
1: Common - 68.9% chance to get
2: Uncommon - 15% chance to get
3: Rare - 10% chance to get
4: Ultra Rare - 5% chance to get
5: Legendary - 1% chance to get
6: Galactical - 0.1% chance to get

Players can boost their chance to get an Uncommon/Rare/UR/Legendary/Galactical item by holding SMI on their account. This is called an SMI Rate Boost. The amount of boost per SMI is yet to be determined.

SMI HOLDER STATUS

By holding SMI, players can achieve a special SMI Holder Status:
1: Fish - needs to be holding 1M SMI
2: Dolphin - needs to be holding 100M SMI
3: Whale - needs to be holding 1B SMI

Fish, Dolphin and Whale players will get access to free daily in-game items as rewards from developers, this includes Token Items. The amount of items and their type depends on the Status.

MOONSHOT VOYAGE

Moonshot Voyage is an upcoming launch title on the SMI Play platform. It's a top-down looter shooter game with a play-to-earn system based on SMI Play Token Items.

Take a role of a space bounty hunter and traverse an unknown galaxy after being sucked into a black hole while trying to find a legendary spaceship - the Moonshot. It won't be an easy task though...

Fight aliens, monsters, ghosts and other deadly creatures along the way to reach the Moonshot and all its secrets.

HOW TO PLAY?

You can play Moonshot Voyage in your web browser. Enjin Wallet app required to link your game account to your Ethereum wallet. Future mobile/standalone ports are possible, but not planned as of now. Keep an eye out for test builds and updates on the SafeMoon Inu Telegram.

Estimated release date: Q1 2022

PLAY TECH DEMO

GAMEPLAY - SPACESHIP

Your spaceship is your hub, where you can prepare your character for an expedition, check your mail, craft items, view your resources, upgrade your weapons and characters etc. There are many different ships available as Token Items which you can customize using furniture items.

GAMEPLAY - EXPEDITIONS

To start an expedition you need to select a character and a destination area. You will then enter an expedition mode. During the expedition you can freely control your character in a top-down view. The goal of an expedition is to explore the area, gather resources, find loot and defeat enemies. Expeditions are the main source of upgrade materials you need for bettering your character and weapons.

There is a very small chance of receiving Token Items during expeditions as random drops, even the rare ones.

You can enter expeditions freely at any time if you have a healthy character and a weapon.

GAMEPLAY - RAIDS

Raids are similar to expeditions, but are slightly longer, harder and end with a boss fight. Raid bosses have a 100% chance to drop a Token Item.

Each raid boss has a set list of available item drops which you can view before starting a raid.

You are limited to 1 raid per day, with some exceptions (like limited-time events or if given a free raid pass).

GAMEPLAY - INTERGALACTIC SPACE STATION

The ISS is a hub where players can interact with each other, have a chat with friends, use the marketplace or view the leaderboards.

GAMEPLAY - COMBAT (1/2)

Combat takes a huge part of Moonshot Voyage. It consists of shooting enemies using your character and a weapon of choice while dodging enemy attacks. Your starting character and weapon will be weak, so you should pay a lot of attention to upgrading and optimizing your setup.

There is a broad variety of guns and weapons available for your characters to equip. You can level up your characters and upgrade your weapons by equipping upgrade modules. An example of an upgrade module is an item that makes your weapon shoot 2 bullets at once instead of 1, increases your shooting speed or just boosts your damage numbers. You have limited upgrade module slots so choose them wisely!

GAMEPLAY - COMBAT (2/2)

By having a high-level character and a good upgraded weapon you will be able to face the hardest expeditions and raids.

To maintain longevity of the endgame, the difficulty and upgrade cap can be increased once in a while.

STADARD ITEMS

Standard items are not placed on the blockchain, you can get them during expeditions, raids or as rewards. There are a few types of Standard Items:
Consumables - items that can be taken with you on an expedition or raid. An example is a healing salve or an item that grants you a timed buff.
Materials - items that can be gathered during expeditions and raids. You can use them to craft better items, upgrade characters and weapons etc.
Miscellaneous - key items to use in raids and expeditions. These items have a variety of uses.

TOKEN ITEMS (1/2)

Token Items are placed on a blockchain, as Fungible or Non-Fungible Tokens. These are much rarer to obtain in game (other than in raids), but can be bought on the marketplace.

There are many types of Token Items:
Characters - randomized NFTs, common type characters are not very powerful, but can be upgraded while rarer characters can have a variety of appearance details, passive or active abilities and statistics.
Weapons - randomized NFTs, they can have different appearances, range, shot speed, bullet types, spread types etc.

TOKEN ITEMS (2/2)

Companions - FTs, small friends you can have with you at all times!
Spaceships - FTs, mostly differ in appearance and size
Furniture - FTs, can be placed in your spaceship and are mostly cosmetic
Upgrade Modules - FTs, have different types and stages (example: Damage Module Level 5 will offer a level 5 boost to damage to your weapon)
Special Tokens - event-tied collectible items, these are very limited and the usage depends on the token

STORY (1/2)

An ancient legend says...

...that once upon a time, people of Earth saw a great, flying fortress traversing through the sky. It would brighten the night sky with its shine so much, that some people couldn't differ the night from day in its presence. The object disappeared behind the moon, never to be seen again. Because of that, people of Earth named the phenomenon - the Moonshot.

Many space travelers and bounty hunters tried seeking for the Moonshot, in a hunt for the unknown. Some said the Moonshot contained sacred treasure, others that it's powered by magic. No matter what the seekers of the Moonshot believed in, they all had one goal: to find the ship.

STORY (2/2)

Tens of decades passed without any sightings of the legendary spaceship. Then, out of nowhere, during one ordinary night, space travelers of all kinds and races looked up in the sky, as it became bright as day again. Like a shooting star, the Moonshot pierced the space above them. This time it was different, though. All of a sudden, a black hole appeared near the spacecraft, sucking it inside. This was the moment all the Moonshot seekers have been waiting for. Each one of them got onto a spaceship of choice and chased the legendary object. Tens of thousands of ships disappearing in the vortex of the black hole...

...and so their story begins...
